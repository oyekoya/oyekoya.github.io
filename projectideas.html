Project Supervision

Project 1: Visualization User Study

Conduct a comparative study of various visualization systems for medical, training, geospatial or any scenario of interest to you. You'll use Unity3D to deploy to various platforms including the smartphone (Android/iOS). The student's work would be focussed on collecting data and analysing the immersion experience of users. Visualization on smartphones can be a highly interactive experience and findings from this study would test the immersion levels compared to highly immersive CAVE and conventional desktops.

Skills Required: Basic knowledge of Unity3D is required however the focus is on the user study.

Project 2: Attention Model Driven Navigation

In this project, you’ll work in Second Life to navigate a user from one location to another in a virtual environment using teleportation technique. The teleport destinations will be driven by a model of attention based on prior work (see my publications list).

Skills Required: Essential skills include C, C++ and an understanding of basic 3D maths.
